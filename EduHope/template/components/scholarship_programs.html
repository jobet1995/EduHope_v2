Future Leaders Grant

For high-achieving students from low-income families pursuing undergraduate degrees.

Benefits

  • Up to $5,000 annually
  • Mentorship program included
  • Community service requirement
  • Academic performance monitoring

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Demonstrated financial need
