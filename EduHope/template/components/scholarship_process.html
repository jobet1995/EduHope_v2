Eligibility & Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for an EduHope scholarship and begin your journey toward educational success.

1

Check Eligibility

Review the requirements for each scholarship program to ensure you qualify.

  • Must be a resident of underserved communities
  • Demonstrate financial need through documentation
  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 2.5 or equivalent
  • Be enrolled or planning to enroll in an accredited institution
2

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documentation for your application.

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Proof of income or financial need
  • Personal statement (500-750 words)
  • Two letters of recommendation
3

Submit Application

Complete and submit your application through our online portal.

  • Create an account on our scholarship portal
  • Fill out the application form completely
  • Upload all required documents
  • Review and submit before the deadline
4

Review & Selection

Our committee will review applications and select recipients.

  • Initial screening of all applications
  • Shortlisted candidates may be interviewed
  • Final selection by our scholarship committee
  • Notification of results via email and portal
5

Begin Your Journey

Start your educational journey with EduHope support.

  • Receive official scholarship award letter
  • Complete any additional requirements
  • Connect with your mentor and support network
  • Begin your studies with financial support
